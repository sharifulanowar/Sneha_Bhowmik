A Voice for the Voiceless

I am Sneha Bhowmik, a dedicated journalism and media student at Daffodil International University with a deep passion for authentic storytelling. I believe that journalism is not just a profession — it is a responsibility to uphold truth, amplify underrepresented voices, and serve the public interest.

My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in print, broadcast, and digital media. I have developed skills in news reporting, feature writing, video production, and social media strategy. I am particularly drawn to investigative journalism and human-interest stories that shed light on social issues affecting communities across Bangladesh and beyond.

Outside the classroom, I actively seek real-world experience through campus media, internships, and independent writing projects. I am driven by the belief that well-told stories can change minds, shift policies, and spark meaningful conversations.

Future Goals

01

Become an Investigative Journalist

My deepest ambition is to become a seasoned investigative journalist, uncovering stories of corruption, inequality, and social injustice. I aim to join a reputed national or international media organization where I can pursue impactful, evidence-based reporting that holds power to account.

02

Pursue International Higher Education

I aspire to pursue a postgraduate degree in Journalism or Media Studies from a globally recognized institution — ideally in Europe or North America. Advanced academic training will strengthen my analytical skills and expose me to diverse global media landscapes and practices.

03

Launch a Digital Media Platform

I plan to create a digital media platform focused on underreported stories from South Asia — particularly those concerning women, climate, and marginalized communities. By blending journalism with multimedia storytelling, I hope to build an audience that values depth over clicks.

04

With the rise of misinformation, I am committed to promoting media literacy among youth in Bangladesh. I envision running workshops, writing educational content, and collaborating with institutions to help the next generation of consumers and creators engage with media critically and responsibly.
